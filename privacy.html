Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how SunNodeLabs handles information for its website and the LineIQ mobile game. We design our products to collect as little personal information as possible.

Information We Collect

LineIQ does not require users to create an account, sign in, or provide personal information to play. SunNodeLabs does not directly collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, contacts, photos, or precise location information through LineIQ.

The SunNodeLabs website may receive basic technical information that browsers and hosting providers normally process, such as IP address, browser type, device type, pages visited, and request timestamps. This information is used for security, reliability, and basic site operation.

Game Progress and Device Data

LineIQ may store game progress, settings, scores, completed levels, hints, and similar gameplay information locally on your device. This data is used to make the game work and remember your progress. It is not used to identify you personally by SunNodeLabs.

Third-Party Services

If LineIQ uses platform services such as Google Play, those services may process information according to their own privacy policies. Future versions of LineIQ may include analytics, crash reporting, advertising, leaderboards, cloud saves, or in-app purchases. If we add those features, this policy will be updated before or when they are released.

Children's Privacy

LineIQ is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to SunNodeLabs, please contact us so we can review and delete it where appropriate.

Data Sharing

SunNodeLabs does not sell personal information. We may share limited technical information only when needed to operate our website, comply with legal obligations, protect users, or maintain security.

Data Retention

Locally stored game progress remains on your device until you delete the app, clear app data, or reset your device. Website technical logs, if retained by hosting providers, are kept only as long as reasonably needed for operation, security, and troubleshooting.

Your Choices

You can remove local LineIQ data by deleting the app or clearing app data through your device settings. You can also contact SunNodeLabs with privacy questions or requests.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact SunNodeLabs at support@sunnodelabs.com.
